Ocean Acidification & Fish Kinematics

Studying how elevated CO₂ disrupts schooling behavior in silversides (Menidia sp.) using a flapping foil array and high-speed video — supported by a MATLAB analysis pipeline I built from scratch to extract swimming kinematics from tracking data.
