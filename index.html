About

Background

I'm an Electrical Engineering student at UC San Diego with a 4.0 GPA, conducting research in Dr. Valentina Di Santo's lab. My coursework in signal processing (ECE 45) and statistics (ECE 109) directly informs how I approach biological data — as structured, measurable signals rather than noise.

Research Vision

I'm drawn to problems that sit at the boundary between disciplines — where engineering tools open new windows into complex biological and ecological systems. Long-term, I aim to pursue graduate research in bio-inspired robotics and intelligent systems applied to climate challenges.
